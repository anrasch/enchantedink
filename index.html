A Day in Nova Polis

A Day in Nova Polis" follows Alex, a courier navigating a futuristic city of contrasts. From the luxury of Zenith Heights to the vibrancy of the Neon District, the industry of the Mechanica District, and the peace of Elysium Gardens, ending in the challenging Dystopia Slums. Through his deliveries, Alex discovers the diverse and human facets of Nova Polis.
